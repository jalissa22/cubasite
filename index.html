Going to Cuba?

For years it was illegal for Americans to travel to Cuba. Things between the US and Cuba didn't get better until 2015 when President Obama tried to restore diplomatic realtions between the 2 nations. For a couple of years, American's were able to travel as tourists to Cuba under a category called "People to People." Although, when President Trump was elected he chose to suspend the normalization and took away the option for American's to travel as tourist under "People to People." But don't worry if you missed the window, there are still ways to visit this amazing country full of culture. Check out the tips below!
